Is this a Free or Paid service?
Towelly is absolutely free to use. The only thing you will need to acquire is the necessary technology, which typically costs about $200.
Attention! You are not going to pay a dime to us: all the technology will be bought in official stores, so that you can make sure Towelly is not a scam service.
Is Towelly globally available?
Yes! You can use Towelly at your hotel, no matter where it is located. Whether in the USA, Turkey, or Europe, we are commited to creating a better experience for you and your guests.
What is innovative about Towelly?
All the interaction between your guests and the service providing them with beach towels will be operated without plastic, via guests' gadgets, which is faster and more convenient, let alone addresses the world's problem of plastic pollution.
